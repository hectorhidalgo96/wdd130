Utah

Utah is an amazing mix of wild, diverse landscapes, from the snow-capped peaks of the Wasatch Mountains near Salt Lake City to the breathtaking red-rock deserts of the south. It's a huge playground for outdoor enthusiasts, with incredible skiing in the winter and endless opportunities for hiking, biking, and exploring national parks like Zion and Arches when the weather is warm. Many people are drawn to the state's family-friendly, community-focused culture
